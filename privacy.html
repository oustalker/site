Privacy Policy

App: Bot Fly Automation Suite
Last updated: October 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Bot Fly Automation Suite (“App”, “we”, “our”, “us”) handles information when you connect your TikTok account and use our publishing tools.

1. Information We Collect

  • OAuth data: TikTok access_token, refresh_token, open_id, token expiry, and granted scopes (e.g., video.upload, video.publish).
  • Publishing metadata: captions, scheduling times, video references (file paths or URLs), and status updates.
  • Operational logs: limited diagnostic information required to keep the Service secure and reliable.

2. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate with TikTok and publish videos on your behalf using TikTok’s official API.
  • Schedule and manage your content, and display publishing status.
  • Maintain Service security, prevent abuse, and comply with applicable rules.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties except TikTok (to fulfill publishing actions) and infrastructure providers (e.g., hosting, databases) strictly as needed to operate the Service.

4. Data Retention

Access tokens are stored securely and refreshed as needed. We retain OAuth tokens and publishing metadata only for as long as required to provide the Service and to meet legal or operational obligations. You may revoke access at any time via TikTok settings, which may disable publishing.

5. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect stored tokens and metadata (e.g., access controls, encrypted transport). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or a copy of your information. Contact us using the details below.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age required by TikTok’s policies in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect data from such users.

8. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We implement reasonable safeguards as required by law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will take reasonable steps to notify you.
